Every little bit helps, every little bit hurts!

  • Be aware of what you're using, and how much; watch that carbon footprint!
  • Use less of stuff; the best way to reduce waste is to be conscious of how much you use!
  • Switch up your commute: try walking/biking/carpooling to work!
  • Use less fossil fuels; replace fossil fuel-run products with things that run on sustainable energy!
  • Buy local; this supports small businesses, and cut out tons of fuel used for shipments!
  • Remember the 3R's; Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle (in that order!)
  • Eat less meat; the high methane levels are attributed to cattle in farm factories!
  • Unplug your electronics; reduce phantom energy by shutting off at night!
  • Plant a garden; it doesn't get much more local than your backyard, and you're producing O2!
  • Spread the word; tell others about these tips, and encourage them to do their part!
